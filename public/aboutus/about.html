A Few Words about us

Our organization is devoted to curiosity, reason and imagination, and we seek knowledge and wonder, without an agenda. We welcome people from every discipline and culture who seek a deeper understanding of the world and connection with others, and we invite everyone to engage with ideas and activate them in your community.
